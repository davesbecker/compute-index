Global Compute & Environmental Risk Index

Live, hourly alternative data tracking regional compute profit margins and physical water consumption benchmarks.

Metric Definitions

  • Hardware Margin ($/hr): The estimated net profit per hour for operating a node. This subtracts the regional cost of commercial electricity and the municipal cost of water cooling from the raw rental revenue.
  • Water Footprint (Liters/hr): The physical environmental risk metric. This calculates how many liters of municipal water evaporate per hour to cool the server, based on local datacenter infrastructure efficiency (WUE).
  • Cost per Million Tokens ($): The true "cost of intelligence." It normalizes hardware prices by taking the hourly rental rate and dividing it by the chip's processing speed to show the actual cost of doing AI work.
  • Raw Hardware Rental Rate ($/hr): The base, gross market price to rent the physical computer chip for 60 minutes on the open marketplace, before any utility expenses are factored in.
